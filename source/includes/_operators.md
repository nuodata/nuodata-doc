
# Available operators

## Simple operators

Operator | Meaning
-------- | -----
eq | equal
neq |  non equal
gt |  greater than
gte |  greater or equal than
lt |  less than
lte |  less or equal than
like |  like (specify a pattern using wildcard ) (e.g. name=like::T, get all the name that starts with T)
ilike |  case insensitive version of like
